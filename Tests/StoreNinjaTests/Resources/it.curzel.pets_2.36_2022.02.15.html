Description

Introducing Desktop Pets for MacOS!

A virtual pet experience like never before.
Get ready to take a trip down memory lane with our pixel art style pets that will bring back the nostalgia of the classic virtual pets you used to love.
Choose from more than 35 adorable creatures and make them your fun companion, or get creative and add your own user-designed, custom pets.
Customize your pet's speed, size, and even simulate gravity to bring your pet to life.
Have your pet walk on windows of other apps, even across multiple monitors.
With the option to launch automatically on login, your pet will always be by your side.

Join our Discord community to share your personalized experiences with other pet owners.

Download Desktop Pets today and enjoy endless fun with your new virtual friend!
